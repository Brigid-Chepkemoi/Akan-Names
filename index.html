Akan names are derived from the Ghanian culture.
Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name'
which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.


Some cool names are;

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday:Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
